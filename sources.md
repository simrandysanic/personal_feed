## Source Quality Notes

- Prefer primary publications for science and research when practical.
- Use reputable summaries for accessibility, but link to the original paper or announcement when available.
- Avoid single-source claims for health, conflict, elections, and financial risk.
- Treat social media posts as leads, not as confirmed sources.
